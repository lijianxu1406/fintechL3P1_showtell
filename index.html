Additive Manufacturing

What is Additive Manufacturing?

Additive manufacturing, often referred to as 3D printing, is a process of creating three-dimensional objects by adding material layer by layer. This technology has revolutionized the manufacturing industry by enabling the production of complex and customized parts with precision and efficiency.

Applications

Additive manufacturing has a wide range of applications across various industries, including aerospace, automotive, healthcare, and more. Some common applications include rapid prototyping, custom medical implants, and lightweight aircraft components.

Benefits

Some of the key benefits of additive manufacturing are:

  • Reduced material wastage
  • Customization and personalization
  • Shorter production lead times
  • Complex geometries achievable
